Having a wall and two ideal, perfectly elastic objects with a given mass we can get given π number approximation. Explanation link.



The mass of the left block is greater than the right one with a given formula: 

mass_right_block = 1
mass_left_block = 100 ^ (k-1)

The result is k-number of π digits approximated.

Try it yourself! You can make many animations at the same time 😉
*works fine on Chrome/Edge/Opera only

Number must be greater or equal 1 and less or equal 8
